Meet the Creator

Hi, I’m Anthony Mercer. I’m passionate about digital marketing, website design, and helping businesses grow. My goal is to become an expert in digital marketing, build user-friendly websites, and be someone others look to for knowledge and solutions. I want to create a cost-effective and universal design company that makes high-quality services accessible to everyone. Whether it’s designing websites or improving search engine rankings, my focus is on delivering value to my clients. I’m skilled in Photoshop, Illustrator, and all Adobe products. I also have strong expertise in Excel, PowerPoint, website design, and SEO management. Throughout building my portfolio and preparing to complete an internship with UAT’s marketing department, I have applied several key digital marketing objectives that have strengthened both my skills and confidence in the field. By studying inbound marketing and SEO strategies, I learned how evolving market trends influence how content is discovered and engaged with online. I focused on creating content designed to attract and connect with a specific audience, making sure it aligned with branding guidelines while still being engaging and informative. While developing these pieces, I also explored how digital marketing strategies work together, using basic analytics and KPIs to better understand what drives engagement and conversion. Managing and organizing my projects helped me develop leadership qualities, especially when planning campaigns and thinking strategically about messaging and audience impact. Additionally, working with both standard and emerging digital platforms allowed me to gain hands-on experience with the tools commonly used in the industry today. Altogether, these experiences have helped shape my portfolio and prepare me for my internship with UAT’s marketing department. More importantly, they have shown me how these skills can continue to grow in the future, giving me the foundation to become the digital marketer I have always wanted to be. Let’s create something great together!

My Community Statement

“Only a life lived for others is a life worthwhile,” Albert Einstein. “You must be the change you wish to see in the world,” Mahatma Gandhi. "Alone, we can do so little; together, we can do so much," Helen Keller. Throughout history, people have made remarks about the importance of community standing together. This paper is aimed toward my goals after graduation and how I plan to implement what I have learned here at UAT toward helping the community and building bonds that matter, focusing on my degree, what I plan to accomplish within my community, and how my experience at UAT has helped guide me toward these goals. I am graduating with a degree in Digital Marketing, and within this industry, we aim to help people build businesses through marketing endeavors. What makes me unique is my ability to adapt to hard situations and thrive in them. For example, I am in college full time, juggling a full-time and part-time job while also fathering 5 kids. This adapting was my push to get into college and choose my degree path, a path that not only takes dedication but has competition to show ability and skill. I expect to learn ways to exploit this uniqueness and use it to my advantage. Influences during my time here at UAT have been Professor Valenzuela, Dr. Pike, Professor Hromas, Professor Portillo, Professor Smith, my wife, my bosses at my internship, and the student services team. All these amazing people have driven me, taught me new concepts, and influenced me in ways I will be able to utilize throughout life. My experience through my undergraduate degree has helped me think about my community and the world in a multitude of ways. One of the ways that it has helped me think about my community is how I can be influential in the way I conduct myself on social media. This is not only a large community that I can reach, but it is a community that is ever-changing and adapting. Outreach within cities and towns has been growing within social media communities and is easier to influence and grow within than ever. This outreach is not limited to local communities either; the span of outreach is global, and the influence all depends on how you portray yourself and adapt to what the people need through what they want to hear, i.e., trends that are forever changing to be what people want to see. Today, that is usually done in 6–30 second videos that change people's perspectives and send them into heaps of research. This research then creates more influence, as people are social creatures, and things that they read and enjoy get shared. The way I conduct myself on social media could, in turn, change people's perspectives, give light to unknown situations, or drive and motivate people as UAT has done for me. My accomplishments at UAT that have prepared me most for my future in community outreach have been my classes with Professor Smith and Professor Valenzuela. Both of these professors have taught me things within the digital marketing field that not only I can use to advance my career after college but can help change and shape communities through marketing strategies. With Professor Valenzuela, I learned everything I need to know to start a career within digital marketing. She has helped me create marketing strategies, research trends, and taught me how to put a presentation for a potential client together. Professor Smith’s Production Studio class has helped me work within a team to create, present, and receive hands-on experience toward how working in a real-world situation would go. Both of these professors helped me accomplish some of my greatest work here at UAT, from the presentations to the papers that were written to showcase knowledge in my degree. Both have prepared me not only for my future career in digital marketing but also helped mold a path for involvement in my community. I plan to utilize these accomplishments to help small businesses grow within my community and larger businesses grow around the world. I want to contribute to small and large advertising groups that are trying to make differences in the world, starting within my small community and working toward bigger and better things. The research I've conducted is limited, but it has been very helpful in finding out what I want to do, just not where I want to start. The things that I've learned from it are that I want to make a difference, I want that difference to grow, and I want to learn as I go along. This research has shown me exactly how making a difference can change not only my life but everyone who is affected by that difference. Some non-academic experiences that have contributed to this awareness are my work within my local food bank, my work within a Section 8 community, and some other community experiences that I have seen make a difference. These things have contributed to the way that I view not only people in need but also the people who are trying to make the difference by helping in any way they can. This is a big thing for a lot of people because not only do a lot of these people want to make a difference, they want to see that difference change people's lives. This has influenced the way that I look at the world and has influenced the way that I want to make a difference in the world. Digital marketing has a lot of influence not only toward people trying to buy things, but it can be used to show people how they can make a difference and how people are making a difference in small and large ways. This is how I want to change the world in a positive way—by showing people how they can make a difference and making it into a very influential social media, advertised, and public thing. All in all, everything that I have learned here at UAT has prepared me not only for my career in digital marketing but has shown me what I can use my degree for to help my community in small and large ways. Eventually, I want these small and large things that are contributed to my community to influence the world.
